About

We're combinding engineering, design, manufacturing, and distribution expertise from the automotive and tech industries to create an eco-friendly, affordable solar water pumping system to help subsistence farmers in developing nations across the globe.

Our Open Source Commitment

Unlike other pump makers, we aren't trying to make a profit or protect our designs, instead we are sharing our process, designs and products with all those interested in solving the issues of water access worldwide.
