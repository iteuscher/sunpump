Pump Info

The Sun Pump is a radically affordable, enviornmentally-friendly, solar water pumping system.

Overview

Driven by motors from Encap Technologies, an industry leader in automotive manufacturing, the Sun Pump channels solar energy through its 24V panels to deliver up to a half liter of water every minute. The solar panels can also be used to charge a mobile phone or car battery. At only 2 inches wide, the pump is able to fit into the narrowest of wells and bring water where it is needed most.

Key Features
  • Lowest price for a reliable solar pump on the market
  • 2 inch wide pump diamater (fits even in very narrow wells)
  • Flow rate of 1/2 liter per second (1,800 L/hr)
  • Can pull water from 7-20 meter depth
  • 1 or 2 24V panels to provide 40-80W
  • Easy to remove and replace the pump impeller
  • Fits into easy-carry backpack
  • Can charge mobile phone and car battery
