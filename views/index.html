Why a Solar Pump?

Throughout the world, many communities have limited access to water. Many rural villages rely on diesel-generators to power electric water pumps (despite their high maintenance and diesel fuel requirements) or rainfall (despite its unpredictablity) for their livelihood.

Solar Pumping (using solar panels to power a water pump) provides a sustainable, cost-effective solution for many rural communities worldwide. By harnessing solar power, farmers will save money, protect the enviornment, and set themselves on a path to a better future.
